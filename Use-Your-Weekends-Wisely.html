Using Weekends Wisely, Extended

While the weekends are great ways to catch a breather from everything going on it is important to spend your time the right way. This doesn't mean don't have fun with friends with friends and family but it does mean that you should dedicate some or even most of your time to getting a jump on the week by studying or completing other work.

Here is a list of wise things to do over the weekend:

  • Spend some time planning out your week
  • Give your mind a few breaks
  • Get a start on homework or projects
  • Study during the weekends to save time during the week
