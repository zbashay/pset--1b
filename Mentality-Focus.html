Mentality/Focus

Having a positive mentality in races, practices, and even during school will improve your attitude and your performance in more ways than one. Your overall mentallity can change race results and will boost the morale of everyone around you.

Here is a list of ways to overcome negative thoughts in a race:

  • Think about the finish line despite the distance left to run
  • Run for something greater than yourself
  • Think about how far you've come
  • Have fun! All runners are in this together!

Here are the top three things to focus on while running:

  1. Breathing
  2. Form
  3. Feelings(and how they can be turned into motivation to run)
