Endurance, Extended

As previously mentioned, Endurance plays a very large role in a runners life. The definition of endurance is "the fact or power of enduring an unpleasant or difficult process or situation without giving way."

Here are a list of exercises that can help build endurance for runners:

  • Be consistent
  • Do more long runs
  • Tempo runs
  • Eating right
  • Taking time to recover
